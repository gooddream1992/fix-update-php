              <p>Eighty percent of America's cucumber crops go toward making pickles; the other 50 percent is distributed among spas for edible eye coverings. Harvest your senses with today's deal: for $40, you get $80 worth of spa services at <a href="http://spagregories.com/">Spa Gregorie's</a>. This Groupon is only valid at the <a href="http://bit.ly/aykwIm">Rancho Santa Margarita location</a> and includes 15% off retail products purchased at the time of service. </p> 
              
              <p>Voted <a href="http://blog.americanspamag.com/2009/12/american-spa-announces-professional%E2%80%99s-choice-awards/">Favorite Day Spa 2009</a> in <i>American Spa</i>s Professionals Choice Awards, Spa Gregories provides patrons with an assortment of services aimed at pulchritude and pampering. Target tension with the Scandinavian kneadery of a <a href="http://spagregories.com/sptrts/mass.shtml">Swedish massage</a> ($55 for 30 minutes), which draws upon classic European techniques to boost circulation, mollify the moaning of muscles, and impart a sense of calm, and <a href="http://spagregories.com/sptrts/hair_rem.shtml#wax">waxing</a> and <a href="http://spagregories.com/sptrts/hair_rem.shtml#threading">threading</a> provide further skin sprucing. Prep for family hand portraits with a <a href="http://spagregories.com/sptrts/mani.shtml">signature manicure</a>, which compensates clappers for their years of service with 60 minutes of soaking, exfoliating, massaging, and moisturizing, or renovate your head threads with a <a href="http://spagregories.com/salon.shtml">haircut</a> ($70 and up for women, $45 and up for men) capable of turning dowdy dos into artful head toppers.</p> 
              
              <p>Whether you're local royalty or just play the part on TV, a trip to Spa Gregorie's gives visitors the treatment typically reserved for a queen, king, jack, or ace. Plush, earth-toned furnishings, soft spa robes, and a serene ambience provide a quick escape from the pressures of the outside world, especially the weighty atmosphere. The value of this Groupon may be applied toward a <a href="http://bit.ly/aDbKEl">promotional package</a>.</p>
